# Artwork. 

A modern digital painting in the style of Van Gogh's 'Café Terrace at Night' merged with M.C. Escher's 'Relativity' staircases. The cobblestones and cafe tables are glowing digital nodes connected by luminous fiber-optic threads. An infinite, starry digital grid in the background represents distributed cloud silos. Isometric 3D software architecture style, hyper-detailed, cinematic lighting.
